# Identity: Graphite Elegance (formerly Premium)

## Visual Identity Prompt / Style Guide
**Base Shape**: A smooth, perfectly rounded square (squircle).
**Background Color**: Dark charcoal or sleek dark grey (similar to #232323 or #1e1e1e). Not pure black.
**Logo Color**: Pure white.
**Logo Style**: The application's logo should be a minimalist silhouette centered perfectly within the squircle.
**Effects**: The logo should feature a subtle drop shadow or inner depth effect, giving it a slightly raised or embossed feel against the charcoal background.
**Overall Vibe**: Premium, elegant, sleek, professional dark mode.

"App icon for [APP NAME], premium sleek design. A dark charcoal grey rounded square background containing a pure white minimalist silhouette of the [APP NAME] logo perfectly centered. The white logo has a subtle, elegant drop shadow giving it depth. Sleek, professional UI asset."
